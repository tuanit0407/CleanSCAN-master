A simple, smart and efficient document scanner for Android

Clean Scan is a mobile document scanner, easily digitize your paper documents into pdfs. With tons of features, document scanning has never been easier. The best part is, itâ€™s open source with an apache licence.

Features

Documents to PDF

Easily convert your paper documents into digitized into a PDF files, looking sharp and clean.

Organize like a pro

Categorize your documents for improved management and search

Share your scans

Share with your friends via email, whatsapp, messenger or and via link to other social networks

Enhanced image quality

No more low light or bad photos, it can auto-enhance the image quality for best performances

Auto-Edge Cropping

Automatically detects edges from the image, so you don't have to

Extract text from Images

With OCR Support, No more typing, it can automatically extract text from images
